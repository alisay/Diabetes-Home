Quick Diabetes Facts

  • 280 Australians develop diabetes every day
  • It is the fastest growing chronic condition in Australia
  • More than 120,000 Australians have developed diabetes in the past year
  • In 2013, diabetes caused 5.1 million deaths globally.

Symptoms of Diabetes

  • Urinate (pee) a lot, often at night.
  • Are very thirsty.
  • Lose weight without trying.
  • Are very hungry.
  • Have blurry vision.
  • Have numb or tingling hands or feet.
  • Feel very tired.
  • Have very dry skin.
